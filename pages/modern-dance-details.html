Modern Dance Department Details

The Modern Dance Department is responsible for choreographing and rehearsing the dance elements of the production inspired by current day culture. The department is led by directors Darwin Rodriguez, Jacqueline Vo, and Jack Vuong, with pieces choreographed by Jay Hutchinson, Dhanzel Mangona, Phillip Nguyen, and Hailey Lee. Collectively with their department, they will showcase a dynamic and evocative aspect of movement that will captivate the audience, and their performances are always a highlight of past productions.
