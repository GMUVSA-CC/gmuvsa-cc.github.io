Variety Acts Department Details

The Variety Acts department of a theater is responsible for bringing together a diverse range of performers to enhance the production and provide a unique and engaging experience for the audience. Directed by Khai Nguyen, this department will include a wide range of performers such as martial artists, lion dancers, singers, acrobats and many others, who bring their unique talents and showcase the talents of our members on stage.
